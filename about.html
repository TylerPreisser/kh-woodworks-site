About

Down-to-earth custom woodworking, built with professional care.

KH Woodworks is a custom cabinetry and woodworking business serving Hays, Kansas and surrounding areas. The work is practical, detailed, and built around the way each room needs to function.

How the work should feel

Premium does not have to mean stiff.

For most homeowners, custom cabinetry is a big decision. The right builder should help the project feel clearer, not more complicated.

KH Woodworks focuses on fit, function, finish, and honest project conversations. Bring a plan, a photo, a sketch, a saved inspiration board, or just a room that is not working yet.

The goal is simple: custom work that looks like it belongs in the home and holds up to everyday use.

What matters

Trust comes from showing the work and explaining the process clearly.

Homeowners should be able to see the craftsmanship, understand the services, and know exactly how to start a conversation.
