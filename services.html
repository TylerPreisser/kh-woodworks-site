Services

Customized cabinets, furniture, and millwork for your space.

At KH Woodworks, we create customized cabinets and furniture for homes and commercial businesses in the Hays, Kansas and surrounding area.

Kitchen Cabinets

We create custom cabinets utilizing a variety of fine woods including cherry, hickory, ash, maple, oak, and more. We offer a variety of door fronts and customized stains and paint colors.

Millwork

Customized trim might be exactly what you need to bring a finishing touch to your space. With unlimited profiles and shapes, KH Woodworks can create trim that caters to a variety of styles.

Bathroom Vanities

Bathroom vanities are offered with a variety of different style options. Contact KH Woodworks to create the perfect vanity for your space.

Built-In Furniture

We offer a variety of built-in furniture. From library shelving to entertainment centers, we can make your ideas come to life.

Laundry Rooms & Mudrooms

In small spaces, organization is key. KH Woodworks helps bring style and functionality to laundry rooms and mudrooms.

Fireplace Mantels

From farmhouse-rustic to sleek and simple, KH Woodworks will create a mantel that will fit your space perfectly. Whether your fireplace is rock or stone, contact us to discover your options.

Commercial Spaces

Needing trim, cabinetry, shelving, or desks for your business or commercial space? KH Woodworks offers these to companies and businesses around the Hays, Kansas area.

Tables

From kitchen tables to conference tables, KH Woodworks creates functional and beautiful pieces for your space.
