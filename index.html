Our Story

Born from a passion for handmade, high quality, and unique pieces, KH Woodworks strives for excellence in both craftsmanship and quality. Located in Catharine, KS, we desire to bring luxury to the Hays and Ellis County area through customized woodworking and cabinetry. As a small, family business, we see the importance of creating spaces and items that last a lifetime. Contact us to learn more about our process and to see what we can do for you.
